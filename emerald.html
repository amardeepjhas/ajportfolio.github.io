ABOUT EMERALD OUTDOOR

The Emerald brand was created to appeal to a group of professionals that enjoy being active in the outdoors. Emerald Outdoors is not restricted in any way. This demographic engages in outdoor activities such as walking, hiking, and riding two or three times per week on average. Emerald Outdoors' target demographic is environmentally conscious 21- to 48-year-olds men, women, and other. At least once a year, they organise a multi-day canoe or backpacking trip into more remote national park areas. Clothing and equipment bearing the Emerald logo are designed for comfort and style and may be worn in both severe and wild environments. Passionate hikers seek out the Emerald brand to ensure they've purchased high-quality, long-lasting outdoor gear that will keep them sporty and fit while exploring Canada's vast wilderness areas.
