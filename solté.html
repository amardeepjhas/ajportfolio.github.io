ABOUT SOLTÉ

Solté is a self-initiated branding project for a fictional plant-based wellness drink. The goal was to create a fun, healthy, and minimal brand identity inspired by vintage aesthetics, targeting health-conscious young adults who appreciate sustainability and retro design.
